WELCOME!

The National Youth Retreat is dedicated to bringing the young adults of the New Testament Church of God, Jamaica together for four days of worship, prayer, fellowship, and the preached Word. This transformative retreat is a national gathering where young believers can deepen their faith, experience spiritual renewal, and connect with a community of gifted youths growing and going with the gospel.

"Let no one look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in your speech, conduct, love, faithfulness, and purity." 1 Timothy 4:12

PROTECTED.

God shields what is His until it is ready. Like Joash hidden in the temple, the season of hiding is not punishment — it is protection. The gift cannot be released before it is ready.

NURTURED.

Even a king must be taught how to reign. Even a prophet must learn to hear. Growth is necessary. Development is non-negotiable. God plants His gifted in fertile soil so they can flourish.

RELEASED.

Then comes the moment we long for. God appoints the moment when everything aligns. He clears the path so the gift nurtured in silence can speak, move, and transform lives — proclaiming Christ.

Year-Round Ministry

More than a weekend

The retreat is the gathering point — but the National Young Adult Ministry is at work all year long.

Project 01

90 Days Challenge

A virtual experience designed to build spiritual discipline through daily devotionals, fellowship, and prayer over 90 days.

Project 02

Fast Walk Pray Play

A dynamic day of evangelism that combines physical fitness, prayer, and fun-filled team games to strengthen unity and faith across Jamaica.

Project 03

YAM Excursion

A bonding experience where youth travel together to visit a local church, engage in worship, and enjoy a local fun spot.

Project 04

Street Evangelism

Young adults responding to the call to spread the gospel across selected communities throughout the year.

Project 05

All Night Prayer

A powerful night of intercessory prayer, worship, and breakthrough together as a national body.

Project 06

Hospital Visit

A compassionate outreach where youth minister to the sick and elderly through prayer, gifts, and words of encouragement.

Project 07

Smoothie Booth

An entrepreneurial booth at the National Youth Congress promoting health and fundraising for the ministry.

Project 08

Yadel Home Visit

Supporting the children at Yadel Girl's Home through visits, gifts, and presence.

Project 09

Church Project

A hands-on initiative where youth contribute to meaningful improvements within local church communities.

Project 10

Youth Retreat
